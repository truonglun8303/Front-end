Discover some of Morocco’s highlights on this classic journey from the buzzing souks of Marrakesh to the Atlas Mountains’ rich Berber culture and the historic port city of Essaouira on the Atlantic coast.


Staying in three fabulous kasbahs and riads, enjoy a selection of experiences that will give you an insight into centuries of history and culture, from local cooking classes, artisan workshops and guided medina walks to a motorbike tour, following the footsteps of Yves Saint Laurent, and exploring hidden gardens. For the active, there are mountain hikes and a chance to try kite surfing. A fabulous introduction to this ever-enchanting corner of Africa.
